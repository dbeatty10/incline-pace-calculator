Web Calculator Suite

This suite of web-based calculators helps you convert between various paces and gradients. Choose a tool below:

  • Flat Pace to Treadmill Equivalents
    Convert your flat pace into equivalent treadmill paces for various gradients.
  • Hill Pace to Flat Pace Converter
    Convert your pace on a gradient (hill pace) to an equivalent flat pace (GAP).
  • Flat Pace to Hill Pace Converter
    Convert your flat pace into an equivalent pace at a specific hill gradient.
  • Pace to Grade Converter
    Convert equivalent hill and flat paces into potential percentage gradients.
  • Elevation to Gradient Calculator
    Convert a distance (in miles) and elevation change (in feet) to a gradient percentage.
  • Weight-Adjusted Speed Calculator
    Calculate equivalent speed and pace at reduced body weight for performance analysis.
  • MPH to Pace Converter
    Convert speed in miles per hour (MPH) to pace in minutes per mile.
  • KPH to Pace Converter
    Convert speed in kilometers per hour (KPH) to pace in minutes per kilometer.

If you have any questions or feedback about these tools, please feel free to contact the developer.
